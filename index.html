Cloudsplitter Press

Cloudsplitter Press is an independent press founded By Tony Holtzman for the publication of his novels. Its name draws inspiration from the Mohican word "tahawus," meaning "cloudsplitter." Tahawus was the original name of Mount Marcy, a mountain in the High Peaks Region of the Adirondack mountains, and the highest pea in New York State. The Adirondacks feature prominently in The Bethune Murals, and of course, in The Adirondack Trilogy.
