The Strange Malady of Alessandro's Uncle and other stories

The Strange Malady of Alessandro's Uncle

To learn more about his uncle's apparent illness, a journlaist's zeal unleashes a stock bubble.

The Boyfriend

An old man's interest in a young woman is derailed by her boyfriend, but all ends well.

See Jane Run

Were it not for the solidarity of a young girls' soccer team, a benefactor's criticism of one of the players, and a parent's response, could have disrupted the team.

The Umbrella

Two friends who last saw each other in high school arrange to meet late in life. They discover that neither of them has fulfilled the other's expectations.

A Cascading Failure

A prolonged power outage affects the entire United States, revealing how dependent its citizens have become on electricity.

Brahms' Fourth Symphony

Only a Game

You and only you receive warning of an impending natural disaster. Would you escape immediately or warn others? what starts as a game ends in a real situation.

Last Days of Summer

Hiking alone, an elderly man encounters a young threesome whose political allegiance he hates. Contrary to his expectations, they come to his assistance after he takes a bad fall.

A Foot in the Door

Twenty-seven years after he had seduced his technician, a scientist first learns the aftermath and tries to make amends.

The Nobel Prize in Literature

An elderly resident of an assisted living facility claims he has just won the Nobel Prize in Literature. The head nurse enlists a psychiatrist to help her find out.
